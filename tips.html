General Tips for Navigating Campus

  • Use the UCF shuttle system to get around campus faster during peak hours.
  • Check building numbers ahead of time to avoid getting lost.
  • Use the Knightways interactive map to find buildings and rooms with ease.
  • Take advantage of on-campus dining options during breaks between classes.

Transportation Tips

  • Download the UCF Transloc app for real-time shuttle tracking.
  • Use the bike racks located at all major buildings for easy transport.
  • Parking garages tend to fill up quickly, so arrive early to secure a spot.

Study and Campus Resources

  • The John C. Hitt Library is the best place for quiet study sessions.
  • Reserve study rooms ahead of time during exam periods to ensure a spot.
  • Take advantage of campus tutoring services for extra academic support.
